What We do.

We provide training, resuorces and support in the field of early childhood. Providing care for children, such as setting schedules and routines, groomimg, feeding, changin diapers, and cleaning rooms and toys. Developing and encouraging age-appropriate learning and socialization to ensure children learn basic skills and concepts, such as communication, manners, sharing, etc.


Where we work.

We are from Cape Town in a rural area. Based in Khayelitsha, Nkanini. We saw that place as a need especially for children because that place was a new place which many people went there to have homes. So we thought of having a daycare centre there to take good care of children and also teach them what they should know.
